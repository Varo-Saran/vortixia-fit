Ab cramping is often caused by muscle fatigue + lactic acid buildup. The solution: controlled movement, slow tempo, no rushing between sets, and stay well hydrated before training. Progress by adding reps weekly, not weight. Never train abs to failure at this stage.

Hip thrust is the highest-activation glute exercise in EMG studies. For desk workers, sitting shortens the hip flexors and neurologically inhibits the glutes — the glute bridge in your warm-up and the hip thrust finisher directly counteract this. Expect visible results in 4–6 weeks of consistent training.
